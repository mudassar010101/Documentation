Zoho CRM

The Zoho CRM integration lets NexCognit send captured leads directly into your Zoho CRM account. This guide walks through connecting Zoho CRM to NexCognit and confirming that leads sync correctly.

Prerequisites

  • Active NexCognit account.
  • Active Zoho CRM account.
  • Permission to authorize third-party integrations.

Step 1 — Open Integrations

Open the Integrations section in NexCognit to begin connecting Zoho CRM to your workspace.

Step 2 — Connect Zoho CRM

Select Zoho CRM from the list of available integrations to start the connection flow.

Step 3 — Connect Account using Composio

NexCognit uses Composio to manage the secure connection to Zoho CRM. Choose Connect Account to continue.

Step 4 — Sign in to Zoho CRM

Sign in with the Zoho CRM account you want to connect to NexCognit.

Step 5 — Grant Permissions

Review and grant the permissions NexCognit needs to access your Zoho CRM data.

Step 6 — Connection Successful

Once authorization completes, NexCognit confirms that the connection to Zoho CRM was successful.

Step 7 — Verify Connected Status

Return to the Integrations section in NexCognit and confirm that Zoho CRM now shows as connected.

Step 8 — View Leads in Zoho CRM

Open Zoho CRM to confirm that leads captured in NexCognit are appearing as expected.

How Zoho CRM Integration Works

Once Zoho CRM is connected, leads captured by NexCognit are sent to your Zoho CRM account automatically. This keeps your sales team working from a single source of truth without needing to manually export or re-enter lead data.

Things to Know

  • Keep your Zoho CRM credentials secure and do not share them outside your organization.
  • If the connection is revoked or expires in Zoho, you will need to reconnect it from the Integrations section.
  • After connecting, confirm that new leads appear correctly in Zoho CRM before relying on the integration for production use.

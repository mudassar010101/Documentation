Channels

Channels are the communication sources that customers use to reach your business through NexCognit. By connecting a channel, you enable customer conversations to flow into the platform and be handled in a consistent way.

Channels overview

NexCognit supports several customer communication channels so your team can meet customers where they already are. Once a channel is connected, messages can be managed inside NexCognit and Iris can respond automatically based on your configuration.

What Channels are in NexCognit

Channels are the entry points for customer conversations. They connect your business messaging tools to NexCognit so incoming messages can be received, reviewed, and handled in one place.

Why channel connections matter

Connecting a channel is important because it allows customer messages to reach the right place and be processed quickly. A connected channel makes it easier for your team to manage interactions and for Iris to assist with replies.

What happens after a channel is connected

  • Customers can message through that channel.
  • Iris can respond automatically based on the configured behavior.
  • Conversations can be managed from NexCognit.

Supported channels

  • Website Chat: Add a chat widget to your website so visitors can start conversations directly from your site.
  • Telegram: Connect a Telegram bot so customers can reach your business through Telegram.
  • Facebook Messenger: Connect your Facebook Page so Messenger conversations can be handled in NexCognit.
  • WhatsApp: Connect WhatsApp as a customer communication channel for inbound messages.
  • Instagram: Connect Instagram messaging so conversations can be managed through the platform.

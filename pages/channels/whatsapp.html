WhatsApp

WhatsApp lets your business receive and respond to customer conversations through Meta’s messaging platform. This guide walks through the setup process from the initial connection to the final activation inside NexCognit.

Prerequisites

  • You already have access to a NexCognit workspace.
  • You have a Facebook account and access to the relevant Meta business account.
  • You are ready to connect a WhatsApp Business account and test it after setup.

Step 1 — Open the WhatsApp setup entry

Open the WhatsApp channel section in NexCognit to begin the connection flow. This is the starting point for linking your business account to the platform.

Step 2 — Connect with Facebook

Choose the option to connect the channel using Facebook. This begins the Meta-based setup needed for WhatsApp Business integration.

Step 3 — Open Facebook login

Continue through the login flow and open the Facebook sign-in step for the business account you want to use.

Step 4 — Continue the Facebook login process

Follow the Facebook login prompts until the connection flow is ready to continue.

Step 5 — Select the business page

Select the Facebook Page that will be associated with your WhatsApp Business setup.

Step 6 — Select the business ID

Choose the business account or business ID that should own the WhatsApp connection.

Step 7 — Choose the WhatsApp Business account

Select the WhatsApp Business account that will be used for customer conversations.

Step 8 — Create a WhatsApp Business account if needed

If a Business account is not already available, create one before continuing with the setup.

Step 9 — Continue account creation

Continue through the prompts to finish creating the WhatsApp Business account.

Step 10 — Choose a business category

Select the category that best describes your business so the account can be configured correctly.

Step 11 — Select the industry category

Choose the industry category that matches the nature of your business.

Step 12 — Select the other category option

If your business does not fit a common category, choose the appropriate alternative option.

Step 13 — Continue after category selection

Proceed to the next step after confirming the business category details.

Step 14 — Add a new phone number

Choose to add a phone number that will be used for the WhatsApp Business account.

Step 15 — Enter the phone number

Enter the phone number that should be associated with the WhatsApp Business account.

Step 16 — Select the country code

Choose the correct country code before continuing with number verification.

Step 17 — Open the phone number field

Open the field where the phone number is entered so the setup can continue.

Step 18 — Enter the phone number again

Complete the phone number entry and confirm that it is the intended business number.

Step 19 — Continue after phone number entry

Proceed once the phone number has been provided and verified as the correct value.

Step 20 — Open the verification code field

When prompted, open the verification code field so the number can be confirmed.

Step 21 — Enter the verification code

Enter the code sent to the phone number to verify the WhatsApp Business account.

Step 22 — Continue after verification

Continue the setup flow after the verification code has been accepted.

Step 23 — Confirm the account connection

Confirm that the account is connected and ready to be used for business messaging.

Step 24 — Finish setup

Complete the remaining setup steps so the WhatsApp Business account is fully available.

Step 25 — Enable the WhatsApp channel in NexCognit

Once the account is ready, enable the WhatsApp channel in NexCognit so it can receive incoming messages.

Step 26 — Continue configuration

Continue through the configuration screen so the connection is finalized inside NexCognit.

Step 27 — Return to NexCognit dashboard

Return to the NexCognit dashboard once the setup is complete. The WhatsApp channel is then ready to use for customer conversations.

How WhatsApp works after setup

Once the WhatsApp channel is connected, customers can message your business directly through WhatsApp and NexCognit can receive and manage those conversations in one workspace. This makes it easier to keep customer communication organized and responsive.

Things to know

  • Use a business phone number that can receive verification codes.
  • Keep your Meta and WhatsApp Business account permissions up to date.
  • After setup, test the channel with a sample message to confirm that the connection works as expected.

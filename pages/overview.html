Overview

NexCognit is an AI sales and lead management platform built around an AI agent called Iris. It helps businesses capture leads, answer customer questions, qualify opportunities, and manage follow-up work from one place.

What NexCognit is

NexCognit gives businesses a practical way to manage customer conversations and lead activity with AI support. It is designed to help teams respond faster, keep conversations organized, and move promising leads forward with less manual work.

How NexCognit works

  1. Connect communication channels such as Website Chat, Telegram, Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, or Instagram.
  2. Add business information into NexCognit using tools such as the Knowledge Base and other configuration areas.
  3. Configure Iris behavior, language, tone, and qualification rules.
  4. Customers send messages through connected channels.
  5. Iris answers questions, qualifies leads, and helps move the conversation forward.
  6. Lead and conversation activity can be monitored from the NexCognit dashboard.
  7. Lead data can be synced to external tools such as Zoho CRM, and meetings can be booked through integrations such as Google Calendar.

What Iris does

  • Answer customer questions using business information and Knowledge Base content.
  • Ask qualifying questions before sharing pricing.
  • Score lead intent and classify leads as Hot, Warm, Cold, or New.
  • Sync lead data into Zoho CRM.
  • Book meetings using Google Calendar.
  • Send follow-up messages or emails.

Main product areas

  • Dashboard: Review business activity, lead signals, and high-level performance.
  • Leads: View and manage people who have contacted the business.
  • Pipeline: Review leads by their current stage or status in the sales journey.
  • Conversations: Manage customer chats from connected channels in one workspace.
  • Agent Config: Define how Iris speaks, behaves, and handles qualification rules.
  • Product Catalog: Store product or service details that support customer conversations.
  • Knowledge Base: Add business content that Iris can use to answer questions more accurately.
  • Media Library: Organize media assets used inside the workspace.
  • Integrations: Connect NexCognit with CRM, calendar, and follow-up tools.
  • Channels: Connect website chat and messaging platforms so customers can reach the business.

Typical customer workflow

A typical NexCognit workflow starts with connecting one or more communication channels, adding business information, and configuring Iris. Once the setup is live, Iris can respond to incoming questions, qualify leads, and support the next steps in the customer journey.
